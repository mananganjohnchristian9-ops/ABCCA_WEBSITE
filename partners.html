Our Partners

Meet the organizations behind ABCCA's success

Proud Partners

Collaborative organizations supporting ABCCA's mission of accessible vocational education

Stoneworks Specialist International Corporation

Strategic Partner & Sponsor

Stoneworks Specialist International Corporation is a leading organization providing expertise and resources to support ABCCA's welding training programs. Their commitment to vocational excellence and industry knowledge helps shape skilled workers who are ready to meet the demands of today's competitive job market.

Local Government Unit of Cavite City

Government Partner & Advocate

The Local Government Unit of Cavite City provides vital institutional support and regulatory oversight for ABCCA's educational initiatives. Their involvement ensures that our training programs align with national standards and serve our community's broader development goals, creating sustainable employment pathways for Cavite residents.

Technical Education and Skills Development Authority

Accreditation & Standards Body

The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) is a key partner in advancing vocational education and training initiatives across the Philippines. Their framework and accreditation ensure that ABCCA's programs meet national competency standards, giving graduates a certification that is recognized and respected across industries nationwide.

Asian Business Cabletow Cooperative Academy

Non-Profit Educational Institution offering free SMAW NC I and NC II certifications in Cavite City.
