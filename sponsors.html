Enrollment Requirements

Everything you need to know before enrolling in our SMAW training program

Eligibility & Requirements

To enroll in our SMAW NC I or NC II training program, please make sure you meet all the basic eligibility requirements below. We're committed to providing quality, free training to motivated individuals ready to pursue a professional welding career.

Basic Entry Requirements

Trainees or students who wish to enroll must possess the following basic entry requirements:

✓ Can communicate either orally or in written form
✓ Physically and mentally fit
✓ With good moral character
✓ Can perform basic mathematical computation

Additional Documentary Requirements

📄

High School Report Card

Form 138 / SF-9 (Photocopy)

High school report card or proof of last school attended. Required for academic verification.

🎓

Learner's Permanent School Record

Form 137 / SF-10 (Photocopy)

Permanent school record or proof of last school attended. Official academic document.

✅

Certificate of Good Moral Character

Character Reference (Photocopy)

Certificate of good moral character or barangay clearance. Required for character verification.

👶

PSA Birth Certificate

Birth Certificate (Photocopy)

Philippine Statistics Authority birth certificate. Required for identity verification.

📸

ID Photos (2×2)

Identification Photos

Four (4) pieces of 2×2 ID photos — recent, with collar, white background.

🖼️

Passport Size Photos

Additional Photos

Four (4) pieces of passport size photos — recent, with collar, white background.

📦

Long Brown Envelope

Document Container

One long brown envelope for storing and submitting all required documents together.

Enrollment Process — Step by Step

01

Submit Requirements

Gather and submit all required documents: ID, birth certificate, photos, school records, and character reference.

02

Fill Out Application

Complete the enrollment application form with accurate personal information and select your preferred program level (NC I or NC II).

03

Attend Orientation

Participate in a mandatory orientation session covering program structure, safety protocols, and a facility tour.

04

Start Training

Begin your SMAW welding journey with expert instruction and hands-on practice in our fully equipped training facility.
