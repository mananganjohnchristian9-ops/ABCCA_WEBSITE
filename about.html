About ABCCA

Asian Business Cabletow Cooperative Academy

What is the Asian Business Cabletow Cooperative Academy?

Asian Business Cabletow Cooperative Academy (ABCCA) is a non-profit educational institution committed to providing accessible vocational training to individuals who aspire to build a career in welding. With a strong focus on practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge, ABCCA offers free training programs in Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC I and NC II.

These programs are carefully designed to teach students both the foundational and advanced techniques of welding, preparing them for real-world applications in various industries. ABCCA's mission is to empower students by combining hands-on experience with safety awareness and professional standards, ensuring every graduate is confident and competent in their craft.

By making high-quality technical education available at no cost, the academy opens doors for personal growth and employment opportunities, fostering a community of skilled, responsible, and motivated professionals ready to contribute to the workforce and society.

Learning Outcomes

Basic Welding Techniques

Safety Procedures

Career Opportunities

After completing SMAW NC I and NC II, graduates can explore a wide range of rewarding careers.

🧰

Welder / Fabricator

Working in construction, shipbuilding, pipelines, manufacturing, or metal fabrication shops.

🔧

Maintenance Technician

Performing welding repairs and maintenance on machinery, equipment, and industrial structures.

🛠️

Pipe Welder

Specializing in welding pipes for industries such as oil, gas, water, and plumbing.

🏗️

Structural Welder

Fabricating and assembling structural steel for buildings, bridges, and infrastructure projects.

🔍

Welding Inspector / QC

Inspecting welds for compliance with safety, quality, and industry standards.

🏢

Entrepreneur

Starting your own welding or metal fabrication business, serving clients locally and beyond.
